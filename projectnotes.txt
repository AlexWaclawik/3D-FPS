Stretch Goal List
	Bare Minimum:
		1) Player Controller
			- weapon
				- sound (X)
				- firing effect
				- fire rate
			- movement (X)
			- HUD
				- crosshair (X)
				- score
				- health
		2) Base Enemy
			- model
			- animations
				- moveset
				- attack set
		3) Level
	Additional Features
		- more weapons
		- more enemy types
		- ammo pickups
		- powerups
		- some sort of extra map movement (jump pads, teleporter, etc)
